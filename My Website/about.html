Sant Longowal Institute Of Engineering and Technology

( Deemed University Under MHRD Govt. of India )

About

         Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering & Technology (SLIET) Longowal has received Deemed University status in 2007 to run various UG and PG courses under its own Academic patterns. The courses of study are organized on semester system (i.e. two semesters in an academic year). Each semester spans for eighteen weeks (i.e. 90 days) excluding end semester examinations. The academic study schemes at each level are balanced with a number of co-curricular activities. Special lectures /invited talks by eminent personalities on varied topic of academic relevance are organized at department level. To motivate and encourage the students, a counseling system is also in-place which involves various faculty members as chief counselor and class counselors. Students are evaluated on a continuous basis throughout the semester by the course coordinators. The medium of instruction is English for all the courses run by the Institute.

Structure

The Academic Section of the Institute is administered by Dean (Academics), Deputy Registrar (Academics), Assistant Registrar (Academics) and dedicated staff members. Academic Section has three distinct cells to deal with the academic matters related to various programs separately. The degree related matters of various under graduate (UG) courses are delt under UG cell, post graduate (PG) and Ph.D. courses under PG Cell whereas the matters related to various certificate, diploma and ICD courses are dealt under Diploma cell.

Academic Rules

All the academic rules, and regulations, study scheme and courses of study for various streams / courses offered by the Institute are approved by its Senate and put on the website for wider circulation and information.
